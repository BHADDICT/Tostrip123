Common Questions

Before You Book

Is this safe? How are friends verified?

Every TosTrip friend goes through a three-step process: government ID verification, a video interview with our team, and a background check. We also read every review after each trip. Any complaint suspends the listing immediately while we investigate.

What's the difference between a friend and a tour guide?

A tour guide follows a fixed route with a group. Your local friend spends the day with only you (or your group), going wherever your conversation takes you. No script, no schedule, no other tourists. They eat with you, introduce you to their friends, and show you the places they actually love.

What's included in the price?

The listed price covers your friend's time. Transport, entrance fees, food, and activities are paid separately as you go ‚Äî this keeps things flexible and fair. Your friend will always know the best-value options and won't take you anywhere overpriced.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes ‚Äî full free cancellation up to 48 hours before your booked time. After that, a 50% cancellation fee applies to respect your friend's time. Rescheduling is always free with at least 24 hours' notice.

What if I don't speak Khmer?

All TosTrip friends speak at least English. Many speak French, Mandarin, Japanese, Korean, German, or Spanish ‚Äî filter by language to find the right match. And honestly, some of the best moments happen without any common language at all.

Can I book for a group?

Yes ‚Äî mention your group size when booking. Groups of 1‚Äì4 pay the same rate per booking. For groups of 5 or more, contact us directly and we'll arrange a group price or match you with multiple friends.
