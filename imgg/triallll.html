🗣️ Essential Khmer Phrases

👋 Greetings & Basics

Hello / Good day
ជំរាបសួរ (Chomreab suor)
Thank you very much
អរគុណច្រើន (Orkun chran)
Please help me
សូមជួយខ្ញុំផង (Som chuoy khnhom phong)
Excuse me / Sorry
សុំទោស (Som tos)
Goodbye / See you later
លាហើយ (Lea haey)

🍽️ Food & Dining

How much does it cost?
តម្លៃប៉ុន្មាន? (Tomlai ponman?)
I'm very hungry
ខ្ញុំឃ្លានណាស់ (Khnhom khlean nas)
Can I see the menu?
ខ្ញុំអាចមើលម៉ឺនុយបានទេ?
Not too spicy, please
កុំឲ្យហាលពេកសូម (Kom aoy hal pek som)
This is delicious!
នេះឆ្ងាញ់ណាស់! (Nih chhnganha nas!)
Can I have the bill?
សូមគិតលុយផង (Som kit luy phong)

🚗 Transportation

Please take me to the airport
សូមយកខ្ញុំទៅអាកាសយានដ្ឋាន
Where is the bus station?
ស្ថានីយ៍ឡាននៅឯណា?
Please stop here
សូមឈប់ត្រង់នេះ (Som chhob trong nih)

🚨 Emergency

Help me please!
សូមជួយខ្ញុំផង! (Som chuoy khnhom phong!)
I need to see a doctor

Please call the police
សូមហៅប៉ូលីស (Som hav polis)
I am lost
ខ្ញុំបាត់ផ្លូវ (Khnhom bat plov)
