2. Service Description

TosTrip connects travellers with local Cambodian host families who offer immersive cultural experiences including, but not limited to:

  • Half-day, full-day, and overnight home stays with real local families
  • Cooking, farming, fishing, weaving, and artisan craft activities
  • Guided cultural excursions (e.g. Angkor Wat visits, boat tours, school visits)

TosTrip acts as an intermediary platform. The cultural experience itself is delivered by the host family. TosTrip is not responsible for the personal conduct of host families beyond the vetting and onboarding process described in Section 6.
