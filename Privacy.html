TosTrip does not sell your personal data to third parties. Ever. We collect only what is necessary to deliver your experience and keep you informed.

1. Who We Are

TosTrip ("we", "us", "our") is a cultural travel platform based in Cambodia that connects travellers with local host families. We operate the website at tostrip.com and any related booking services.

For the purposes of data protection, TosTrip is the data controller of any personal information you provide to us.

We collect the following categories of personal data:

We do not collect sensitive data such as racial origin, religion, health records, or biometric data unless you voluntarily disclose a dietary restriction or medical condition relevant to your experience.

3. Why We Collect Your Data

We collect personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract — To process your booking and deliver the experience you've purchased.
  • Legitimate Interest — To improve our platform, prevent fraud, and maintain security.
  • Consent — To send you marketing emails or newsletters (only when you opt in).
  • Legal Obligation — To comply with Cambodian tax and business regulations.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your personal data may be used to:

  • Confirm and manage your booking or inquiry
  • Send booking confirmation emails and reminders
  • Coordinate your experience with the host family
  • Process payments and optional insurance/transport add-ons
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Send you post-experience review requests (one email only)
  • Improve our platform based on anonymised usage analytics
  • Comply with legal or regulatory requirements

We will not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces significant legal effects.

5. Data Sharing

We share your data only where necessary and with appropriate protections in place:

  • Host Families: We share your name, booking date, number of guests, dietary needs, and any special requests with the relevant host family to prepare your experience.
  • EmailJS: We use EmailJS to transmit booking and message confirmations. Your submitted form data passes through their servers to deliver emails.
  • Insurance Providers: If you opt in to travel insurance, relevant booking data is shared with Forte Insurance (Cambodia) Plc. or AIA (Cambodia) Life Insurance Plc. to facilitate your policy.
  • Legal Authorities: We may disclose data if required to do so by law or valid government request.
We do not share, sell, or rent your personal data to advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties for their own commercial purposes.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Essential cookies: Enable core site functionality (no consent required).
  • Analytics cookies: Understand how visitors use our site (anonymised). We use Google Analytics or equivalent.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your language and filter selections.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling analytics cookies will not affect your ability to book.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes it was collected for, including legal and accounting requirements:

  • Booking records: 7 years (Cambodian tax law requirement)
  • Message inquiries: 12 months from date of receipt
  • Marketing consent records: Until you withdraw consent
  • Technical/log data: 90 days rolling

After the retention period, your data is securely deleted or anonymised.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations).
  • Restriction: Ask us to stop processing your data in certain circumstances.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw marketing consent at any time by emailing us or clicking "unsubscribe" in any email.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@tostrip.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure, including:

  • HTTPS encryption on all pages
  • Secure, access-controlled email transmission (EmailJS)
  • Limited internal access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular review of our data handling practices

No method of internet transmission is 100% secure. If you become aware of a security concern, please contact us immediately at security@tostrip.com.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Services are directed at adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18. If a child is included as part of a group booking, their data is provided by the responsible adult guest, and handled accordingly.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you by email.

Continued use of our Services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
